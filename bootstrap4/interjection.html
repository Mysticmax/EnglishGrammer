INTERJECTION

Interjections are words that you can use to express a strong sense of emotion or feeling. An interjection is usually just a single word – and unlike any other part of English grammar, it doesn't influence the grammar of a sentence in any way. ... You can use interjections at any time, to add an extra touch of meaning.

Let's begin with some simple example

Ahh!,that feels wonderful.
[आह, यह अद्भुत लगता है।]


Alas!I'm lost in the wilderness.
[काश! मैं जंगल में खो गया हूँ।]

Bah!That was a total waste of time.
[बह! वह समय की कुल बर्बादी थी।]

Wow! That was easy!
[वाह! वह तो आसान था!]

Wait! I am not finished.
[रुकना! मैं समाप्त नहीं हुआ हूँ]

Great, I would love to go to the beach.
[बढ़िया, मुझे समुद्र तट पर जाना अच्छा लगेगा]

Stop! You can't come in here!
[विराम! तुम यहाँ नहीं आ सकते!]

Defination

a word that is the name of a thing, an idea, a place or a person

Types of noun

  1. Common noun.
  2. Proper noun.
  3. Concrete noun.
  4. Abstract noun.
  5. Collective nouns.
  6. Countable noun
  7. mass nouns

Example

e.g: I saw a black zebra at the zoo.
