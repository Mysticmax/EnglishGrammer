PREPOSITION

A preposition is a word or group of words used before a noun, pronoun, or noun phrase to show direction, time, place, location, spatial relationships, or to introduce an object. Some examples of prepositions are words like "in," "at," "on," "of," and "to." Prepositions in English are highly idiomatic.

Let's begin with some simple example

She drove to the store.
[वह दुकान पर चली गई।]


He reads in the evening
[वह शाम को पढ़ता है]

I go to work at 8:00.
[मैं 8:00 बजे काम पर जाता हूं।]

I work on Saturdays.
[मैं शनिवार को काम करता हूं।]

He will be in Toronto for 3 weeks.
[वह तीन सप्ताह के लिए टोरंटो में रहेंगे।]

She will finish her homework by 6:00.
[वह 6:00 बजे तक अपना होमवर्क पूरा कर लेगी।]

He works part time during the summer.
[वह गर्मियों के दौरान पार्ट टाइम काम करता है।]
