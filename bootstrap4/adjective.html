ADJECTIVE

What is an adjective? Adjectives are words that describe the qualities or states of being of nouns: enormous, doglike, silly, yellow, fun, fast. They can also describe the quantity of nouns: many, few, millions, eleven.

Let's begin with some simple example

Your house is older than mine.
[तुम्हारा घर मुझसे पुराना है।]


Tom is the tallest man in the town.
[टॉम शहर का सबसे लंबा आदमी है।]

He is more intelligent than this boy.
[वह इस लड़के से ज्यादा बुद्धिमान है।]

Linda’s hair is gorgeous.
[लिंडा के बाल बहुत खूबसूरत हैं।]

This shop is muchnicer.
[यह दुकान ज्यादा अच्छी है।]

He writes meaningless letters.
[वह अर्थहीन पत्र लिखता है।]

They live in a beautiful house.
[वे एक सुंदर घर में रहते हैं।]

Defination

a word that is the name of a thing, an idea, a place or a person

Types of adjective

  1. Common noun.
  2. Proper noun.
  3. Concrete noun.
  4. Abstract noun.
  5. Collective nouns.
  6. Countable noun
  7. mass nouns

Example

e.g: I saw a black zebra at the zoo.

Meaning in hindi
