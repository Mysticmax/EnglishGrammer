PUNCTUATION

the marks, such as full stop, comma, and brackets, used in writing to separate sentences and their elements and to clarify meaning.

Let's begin with some simple example


Full Stop (.)

We will put a full stop to it.
[ हम इस पर पूर्ण विराम लगा देंगे। ]

Question Mark (?)

Would they make it on time?
[क्या वे इसे समय पर बनाएंगे?]

Quotation Marks (" ")

John said, “I really hate when it’s hot outside.”
[जॉन ने कहा, "जब बाहर गर्मी होती है तो मुझे वास्तव में नफरत होती है।"]

Single Quotation Marks (' ')

Principal Agrees to 'Cabaret' as Senior Play
[प्रधानाचार्य ने 'कैबरे' को सीनियर प्ले के रूप में स्वीकार किया]

Apostrophe (‘)

She's always on time.
[वह हमेशा समय पर होती है।]

Comma (,)

He is a strong, healthy man.
[वह एक मजबूत, स्वस्थ व्यक्ति है।]

Hyphen (-)

This rock-hard cake is absolutely impossible to eat.
[यह रॉक-हार्ड केक खाने के लिए बिल्कुल असंभव है।]

Exclamation Mark (!)

“Stop!” he yelled.
["विराम!" वह चिल्लाया।]

Colon (:)

We knew who would win the game: the Eagles
[हम जानते थे कि कौन खेल जीतेगा: ईगल्स]

Semicolon (;)

I have a big test tomorrow; as a result.
[कल मेरी एक बड़ी परीक्षा है; नतीजतन।]

Parentheses ( )

He gave me a nice bonus ($500).
[उसने मुझे एक अच्छा बोनस ($500) दिया।]

Brackets [ ]

Bill shook hands with [his son] Al.
[बिल ने [उनके बेटे] अल से हाथ मिलाया।]

forward Slash (/)

the teacher noticed that a student had left his/her backpack.
[शिक्षक ने देखा कि एक छात्र/छात्रा अपना बैग छोड़ गया था।]

backSlash (\)

You must write ' \\ ' to put one backslash in the string.
[स्ट्रिंग में एक बैकस्लैश डालने के लिए आपको ' \\ ' लिखना होगा।]

Curly brackets { }

Select your pizza topping {pepper, onion, sausage, tomato} and follow me.
[अपने पिज्जा टॉपिंग {काली मिर्च, प्याज, सॉसेज, टमाटर} का चयन करें और मुझे फॉलो करें।]

Defination

Punctuation is the use of symbols such as full stops or periods, commas, or question marks to divide written words into sentences and clauses.Punctuation is the symbols that you use to divide written words into sentences and clauses.

Types of punctuation

  1. Full Stop (.)
  2. Question Mark (?)
  3. Quotation Marks (" ")
  4. Single Quotation Marks (' ')
  5. Apostrophe (‘)
  6. Comma (,)
  7. Hyphen (-)
  8. Exclamation Mark (!)
  9. Colon (:)
  10. Semicolon (;)
  11. Parentheses ( )
  12. Brackets []
  13. forward Slash (/)
  14. backSlash (\)
  15. Curly brackets { }
