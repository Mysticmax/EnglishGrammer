PRONOUN

A pronoun is a word that is used instead of a noun or noun phrase. Pronouns refer to either a noun that has already been mentioned or to a noun that does not need to be named specifically. There are a number of other types of pronouns.

Let's begin with some simple example

He missed his bus.
[उनकी बस छूट गई।]


Sham hit you.
[शम ने तुम्हे मारा।]

Police will ask them.
[पुलिस उनसे पूछताछ करेगी।]

Ram has invited us.
[राम ने हमें आमंत्रित किया है।]

You are running.
[तुम दौड़ रहे हो।]

He is studying.
[वह पढ़ रहा है।]

We are eating pizza.
[हम पिज्जा खा रहे हैं।]

Defination

A pronoun is a word that is used instead of a noun or noun phrase.

Types of pronoun

  1. Personal pronouns.
  2. Possessive pronouns.
  3. Relative pronouns.
  4. Reflexive pronouns.
  5. Intensive pronouns.
  6. Indefinite pronouns.
  7. Demonstrative pronouns.
  8. Interrogative pronouns.
  9. Reciprocal pronouns.
  10. Distributive pronouns.
